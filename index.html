Short Info About Norway

Norway, a Scandinavian country, is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and modern lifestyle.

  • Capital: Oslo
  • Official Language: Norwegian
  • Currency: Norwegian Krone (NOK)
  • Time Zone: CET (Central European Time)

Norway is famous for its breathtaking fjords, majestic mountains, and the mesmerizing Northern Lights. It's a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, offering opportunities for hiking, skiing, fishing, and kayaking. Additionally, Norway boasts a vibrant cultural scene, with a strong emphasis on music, art, and literature.

Famous Norwegians

  • Edvard Munch: Renowned painter known for his iconic work "The Scream."
  • Roald Dahl: Author of beloved children's books like "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory" and "Matilda."
  • Bjørn Ulvaeus and Benny Andersson: Members of the legendary pop group ABBA.

Norwegian Festivals

Norway hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year, celebrating music, culture, and nature.

  • Øya Festival: A popular music festival featuring both Norwegian and international artists.
  • Bergen International Festival: A multidisciplinary arts festival showcasing theater, music, dance, and visual arts.
  • Roskilde Festival: A large music festival held in Denmark, but with a significant Norwegian presence.
